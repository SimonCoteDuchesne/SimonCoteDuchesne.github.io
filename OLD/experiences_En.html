Volunteer


WonderJam is an event that was happens twice a year, once in the fall semester and another in the winter. A game jam is a competition where teams have exactly 48 hours (or another time constraint) to design a playable and fun game with design constraints. For exemple, the theme is usually given by the event organisers exactly when the event starts. The Wonderjam is the game jam organised by the UQAC students in the AEMI student association. I really loved my first 2 experiences as a participant of the Wonderjam and so I decided to help. I was interviewed about the event on various local media stations like Radio-Canada, KYK radio X and TVDLDERY (video). I was also part of the team setting up the room and helping participants finding their spot.

Webrio is a web development agency focused on website creation and management. As an intern, I layed the bases for a social media type of web app. Using HTML 5 and PHP, I created some databases and a basic user interfaces.



Unchained


Teaming up with 3 devs from UQAC, 4 artists from Centre NAD and 2 sound designers, we partook in the Ubisoft Game Lab in 2017. There were major design constraints imposed as part of this 10 week game jam that also were worth 3 university credits. The theme was "playing with time" and they imposed an asymetric multiplayer gameplay as part of the challenge. This was the most intense and amazing experience of all my bachelor's degree. I worked a little bit on the gameplay, but mostly on the user interface and user experience.

We were nominated for:
- Best technical and innovative challenge
- Best user experience
- Best prototype
We won:
- Best technical and innovative challenge

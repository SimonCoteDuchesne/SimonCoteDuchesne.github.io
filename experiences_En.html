Volunteer


WonderJam is an event that was happening twice a year, once in the fall semester and another in the winter. A game jam is a competition where teams of game designers have exactly 48 hours (or another time constraint) to design a playable and fun game with design constraints. For exemple, the theme is usually given by the event organisers when the 48h starts and never before. The Wonderjam is the game jams organised by the UQAC students in the AEMI student association. I really loved my first 2 experience as participant of the Wonderjam and I decided to help the organisation of further WonderJam during my bachelor at UQAC. I was interviewed about the event on various local media station like Radio-Canada, KYK radio X and TVDLDERY (video). I was also part of the team setting up the room and helping participants finding their place.


President


As part of my final 2 semesters in the university, I was elected president of the WonderJam team. As the president, I had to build a solid team of volunteers to help build the event twice. We also won the price of the best project that year as elected by my peers as part of the "gala de l'implication MAGE-UQAC 2016-2017."

Various projects

Saguenay Média, now Webrio, is a web development agency focused on website creation and management. As an intern, I layed the bases for a social media type of web app. Using HTML 5 and PHP, I created some databases and a basic user interface.



Unchained


Teaming up with 3 programmers from UQAC, 4 artists from Centre NAD and 2 sound designers, we partook in the Ubisoft Game Lab in 2017. There were major design constraints imposed as part of this 10 week game jam that also were worth 3 university credits. The theme was "playing with time" and they imposed an assymetric gameplay for 2 players as part of the challenge. This was the most intense and amazing experience of all my bachelor's degree. I worked a little bit on the gameplay, but mostly on the user interface and user experience.

We were nominated for:
- Best technical and innovative challenge
- Best user experience
- Best prototype
We won:
- Best technical and innovative challenge

About me

When I was a programmer, I also volunteered as a tutor. I helped elementary school kids facing difficulty learning with their homeworks. It was even more rewarding than my full time job. Seeing the kid's reaction and improvement along with the support of the parents was a great feeling. When I got fired as a video game programmer, I decided to change career and to go into education. Since then I've been a full time teacher with immigrants teenagers and with kids on the autism spectrum. I've done paid online tutoring and part time substitute teaching. I am also studying part-time the masters leading to the teaching permit.
